Cost Breakdown:
  • Room Cost: ₹20,000 per night. The room charge is calculated by multiplying the number of nights by ₹20,000.
  • Conference Hall: ₹5,000 per day. If you use the conference hall for 1 day, the cost will be ₹5,000.
  • Swimming Pool: ₹3,000 per day. The pool cost is calculated by multiplying the number of days you use it by ₹3,000.
  • Gym: ₹1,800 per day. Similarly, the gym cost is calculated by multiplying the number of days by ₹1,800.
  • Food Cost: You can enter the total cost of the food you ordered during your stay.

For example: If you book a room for 3 nights, use the conference hall for 1 day, and order food worth ₹1,500, the total cost will be calculated as:

Room Cost (₹20,000 * 3) + Conference Hall (₹5,000 * 1) + Food Cost (₹1,500) = ₹66,500
